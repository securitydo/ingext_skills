## Implementation notes

- **ingext-kql (1.0.1 → 1.0.2)** — re-synced the embedded schema knowledge base from the

  24 tables, adding **CortexAlert**, **CortexIncident** (Cortex), **ZscalerDNS**,

  tables dynamically from the manifest, so no prose/behaviour changes were needed.
  `references/schemas/manifest.json` regenerated (24 tables). Verified with
  `sync_schemas.py --check` reporting the KB up to date.
